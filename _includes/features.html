Features

Fully Air-Conditioned
The hall is fully air-conditioned ensuring comfort for all the guests.
Rooms
Separate rooms available for bride and groom. Ideal for a wedding and Reception day.
In-House Decorator
Our in-house decorator supporting all your decoration requirements. A dedicated team will work with you to ensure you have the beautiful event.
In-House Caterers
Our in-house team are some of the best reviewed caterers of Kolkata. We provide options to select your own caterers for the best food service on the event day.
In-House Cleaning & Support
Our in-house cleaning and support staff ensures that our guests are supported in all their needs and troubles. Our dedicated team will be available all times on the day of the event.
In-House Security
The area is protected by 24x7 CCTV camera and is monitored to ensure safety within the premises.
Age Friendly Area
Ground floor facility helpful for old aged and disabled people.

Ample car and two-wheeler parking area available with support of our in-house valet. More on-road parking available.

Separate Toilet facility for Men and Women. Our support team will ensure that the toilets are cleaned and sanitized.
Network Coverage & Wifi
The banquet hall area is supported by strong cellular network coverage. The area is also WiFi enabled only limited to the event day guests.
Local Transportation
Easy access to local transportation (bus, auto, toto, rickshaw), private transportation (yellow cab, Ola, Uber), train station and metro (within 5 - 10 minutes), and airport (within 15 - 20 minutes).
